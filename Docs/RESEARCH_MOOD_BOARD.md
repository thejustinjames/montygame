## Aesthetic Direction Summary

### Overall Vibe
🎨 **Bright, Playful, Retro-Futuristic**
- Colors: Vibrant primaries + pastels (reds, blues, greens, purples, oranges)
- Style: Hand-drawn, chunky lines, friendly features
- Tone: Non-threatening, whimsical, encouraging
- Era: 1950s–1980s sci-fi mixed with modern indie game charm

### By World

#### World 1: Dino Jungle 🦕🌴
**Primary References:**
- `trex_getoblaster.jpeg` (playful dino with boombox — **captures the vibe perfectly**)
- `trex.jpeg`, `trex2.jpeg`, `dinosours.jpeg` (character variety)
- `backdrop.jpeg` (layered environment depth)

**Aesthetic Goals:**
- Friendly, curious dinosaurs (not scary)
- Lush green colors with tropical flair
- Ancient ruins suggesting history without darkness
- Playful obstacles (rolling rocks, swinging vines, mud)

#### World 2: Space Station 🤖🚀
**Primary References:**
- `pngtree-robot-toys-friendly-robots-waving-retro-sci-fi-design-bright-colors-png-image_17663791.png` (**PRIMARY MATCH** — bright, friendly robots in primary colors)
- `r2d2.jpeg`, `c3po.jpeg` (iconic, trustworthy bots)
- `codey.jpeg` (modern cute robot)
- `space.jpeg` (cosmic backdrop)
- `spaceship.jpeg` (tech aesthetic)

**Aesthetic Goals:**
- Bright blue/purple color scheme
- Friendly robots as NPCs and obstacles
- Tech without being intimidating
- Laser beams and floating platforms (zero-gravity vibes)

#### World 3: Rocket Canyon 🚀🏜️
**Primary References:**
- `rocket.jpeg`, `rocket2.jpeg` (obstacle design)
- `ufo.jpg` (hyperspace jump visual)
- `space.jpeg` (desert + sky backdrop)
- `Star-Destroyer_ab6b94bb.jpeg` (sense of epic scale)

**Aesthetic Goals:**
- Orange/red desert palette
- Retro 1950s rocket imagery
- High-speed, flying obstacles
- Ancient meets futuristic (portals, rockets in ruins)

## Character Design Direction

### Main Characters (Custom-Drawn)
- **Dino:** Colorful stripes (blue/green), orange spikes, heart-shaped eye, playful energy
  - Inspiration: `trex_getoblaster.jpeg` (playful attitude) + custom child art
- **Cat:** Orange/brown coloring, blue wings/ears, expressive eye, quick-witted energy
  - Inspiration: Custom child art (unique, character-driven)

### NPCs & Enemies (To Design)
- **Space Station Robots:** Based on `pngtree-robot-toys-...png` (bright, waving, friendly)
- **T-Rex Boss (World 1):** Based on `trex.jpeg`, but non-violent (dodge, not fight)
- **Robot Guardian (World 2):** Based on `r2d2.jpeg` + `c3po.jpeg` (helpful but strict)
- **Rocket Boss (World 3):** Original design inspired by `ufo.jpg` + `Star-Destroyer_ab6b94bb.jpeg`

## Notes for Asset Team / Animator

1. **Priority:** Hand-drawn Dino & Cat character sprites (drives all other design)
2. **Tone:** Keep all designs bright, friendly, and non-threatening (for 5–7 year-olds)
3. **Retro Inspiration:** Lean into 80s sci-fi aesthetics (Donkey Kong era, Saturday morning cartoon vibe)
4. **Color Consistency:** Use derived palettes; avoid muddy or muted colors
5. **Playfulness:** Characters should be expressive and react positively to player actions (celebrations, encouragement)
